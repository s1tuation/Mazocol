Mazocol Farming Services

Who we are:


Mazocol Farming Services MFSs deals with agricultural aspects such as crop
and animal farming. Shifting towards high-valued agricultural commodities
which opens opportunities for farmers, particularly small-scale and emerging
farmers to branch out towards commodities possessing high capabilities for
significant returns on land, labour and capital.

Our vision and mission:


To help restore and maintain vibrant rural communities and enable current and
prospective farmers to progress beyond subsistence and emerging agriculture.
To disseminate knowledge and skills to our small-scale and emerging farmers,
empower youth and women, and other prospective farmers across the Eastern Cape
Province to run agribusinesses that are both sustainable and profitable in
order to ease poverty by creating jobs and ensuring food security for our
nation. This will be achieved through training, mentoring and direct work
application.


Agricultural consultancy

Mazocol Farming Services offers expertise agricultural consulting services in Livestock Production, Crop Production and Agricultural Economics.


Training and development


Providing non- accredited agricultural training in Farm Planning, Animal
Production and Crop Production. Training is provided by agricultural scientists
and professional consultants.
